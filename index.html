*** Jean Baptiste Beaubien Elementary School ***

Campus life

School Size

The school is 3 floors and includes a basement. Every class has a Promethean board and each student has their own personal chromebook provided by the school.

Artistic Skills

There are 3 class to show studenst their artistic skills, Music, Art, and Dance. A student is awarded every year for being the best in the 3 classes.

Curriculum

The curriculum is great in developing students. There is an after school program and the are clubs included in the after school. The school hosts a spelling bee and geography bee every year and students have gone to state and beyond.

School Achievments

Awards
Awards

The school has won multiple awards. The school clubs also compete in comptitions. Chess club has won several awards. Math club also has a lot of awards.

B-Show
B-Show

The school hosts the B-Show which is a performance held at about the end of the year. Any student that is accepted from the auditions can preform. There are 3 B-Show performances held each year. 2 for the school, and 1 for the parents. There could be more. Due to Covid-19, there was a virtual performance.

Science Fair
Science Fair

The Science Fair happens every year. 3rd-12th grade does the Science Fair. It is optional to do it in some other schools. People have gone to State competitions.
